FAQ

Reservation

If you wish to book with a specific artist, the booking process may vary depending on the artist.

You can use the Reservation tab on our website or contact the artist directly.

Once you reach out, we will confirm your desired design, size, placement, and preferred date. After the consultation, we will provide payment instructions for the booking deposit.

A deposit is required to secure your appointment. Deposits are non-refundable in case of a client’s personal reason.

However, if the artist is unable to proceed due to personal circumstances, the deposit will be fully refunded.

Before Your Appointment

Please avoid excessive drinking or intense exercise the day before your session. Keep the tattoo area clean and, if possible, shave the area in advance.

If your skin has wounds, rashes, severe dryness, or sunburn, the session may not be possible. We recommend waiting until your skin has fully healed.

Artists will create a design based on inspiration pictures and keywords that you send. Adjustments can be made to suit the artists' style.

Tattoo Procedure

This depends on the size and detail of the design.

Small tattoos typically take about 1–2 hours, while medium to large pieces may take several hours.

Please consult with your artist for a more accurate estimate.

Our studio does not use numbing cream.

While it may help reduce discomfort, it does not provide complete pain relief.
Our artists prioritize your comfort, taking breaks as needed to ensure the best experience.
